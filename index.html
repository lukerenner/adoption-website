What Our Life is Like

Life with us is full of energy and affection, with creativity woven into everything we do. We both have big "art hearts", so we love spending time on house projects, playing music, and writing. We can't wait to nurture a child's creative spirit and watch their imagination take off.

Beyond our passion for art, we are an active couple who loves the outdoors. We hike, bike, camp, and stay fit at the gym. We're excited to share our love for nature and fitness.

Travel is another passion we plan to share with a future child. We believe in exposing a child to the world's beauty and diversity.

We want to create a home where a child feels safe, supported, and celebrated for their unique self. As a gay couple, we've learned the importance of feeling like you belong.

Our Promise to You

We know this is a big decision. Where your child grows up shapes who they become and the life they'll have. At the end of the day, we're offering them a life full of love and care. We promise to give them a strong sense of belonging and make sure they always feel safe and supported.

We'll be there to nurture their talents and make sure they have everything they need to grow into the best version of themselves. Every day, we'll honor your child's unique identity and background. Our home is filled with kindness, empathy, and acceptance, and we'll raise your child with these same values. They'll always know their story, the deep love and strength behind your decision, and how grateful we are to be their parents.

Thank you for considering us. We want this journey to feel comfortable for you, and we're here to support you every step of the way. We can't wait to get to know you better and hear more about what the future looks like for you, your child, and all of us together.
